Our vision

Our vision for the bakery and love for baking transpired to be something innovative which can be shared globally. Also to to allow people to share their art of Baking via upload facility.

Our success story

One of the key factors in our bakery success has been its ethos that items taste best when they’ve been freshly baked using exactly the same ingredients and techniques as those used in home baking. For this reason, each and every RMS Bakery branch has its own kitchen and a team of resident expert bakers and cake decorators. This enables the bakery to bake to order and to only serve its customers cakes that have been freshly baked the very same day. The RMS bakery offers a full range of Indian baked treats and desserts including cupcakes, layer cakes, brownies, pies, cheesecakes and much more. It played a lead role in bringing cupcakes and Red Velvet cake to popularity and continues to innovate, introducing new and exciting cupcake flavours through its ‘Monthly Specials’ series. The bakery also caters for customers requiring cakes and cupcakes for weddings, birthdays, events and other celebrations. There is no secret to what makes The RMS Bakery’s cakes and other treats taste so good. We give opportunity to our budding bakers to showcase their talents through UPLOADING THEIR VIDEOS. No matter where a RMS Bakery branch pops up, customers can always be assured of a welcoming environment, friendly staff and the delightful smells and tastes of authentic Indian home baking.
